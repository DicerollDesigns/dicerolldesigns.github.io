Undergrounders on the run

Keller, a gunman-for-hire, travels to Tristalona after he garners some unwanted attention from the Dristrali government and to meet up with an old friend, Blanca, who wants to take him for a big heist that she's been planning. From her description of the job, it was supposed to be a simple heist. Break into the mansion, get a priceless artifact and get out.

Of course, the run doesn't go as planned. Instead of an artifact, the crew finds an unconcious collared girl in it's place. Now with unwanted cargo and pursuers hot on their tail, Keller has to figure out a way to get out of the mess Blanca dragged him into and hopefully find out the truth behind the mansion, the girl and the person who set them all up.

Keller

Bernholdt Keller. An Undergrounder grinding away and beneath the surface of civil society and into the darker parts of the world, sometimes longing to seize the life of normalcy he was denied.

Blanca

One of the most notorious undergrounders in the Espadan Region. She likes to call herself a "radical political activist", fighting for "the little man". Blanca aids the anti-monarchist movements from the shadows in the hopes of turning her country into a more free and liberal society.

The Girl

Rescued by Keller and Blanca, nobody really knows much about the girl. With her collars magically rendering her mute and draining her of magic, she spends most of her time hidden away in "The Whiskey Dip", hoping that her former captors don't find her.
